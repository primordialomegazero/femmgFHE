\begin{equation}
\lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{N_{n+1} - N^*}{N_n - N^*} = \phi^{-1}
\end{equation}

where $N^* \approx 341.5$ is the observed Banach fixed point.

This is an empirical observation. The mathematical derivation from RLWE first principles remains an open problem.

\subsection{The φ-Riemann Connection (Speculative)}

\begin{equation}
r_n = \frac{\gamma_{n+2} - \gamma_{n+1}}{\gamma_{n+1} - \gamma_n}
\end{equation}

Key findings:
\begin{itemize}
    \item $\phi/2 \approx 0.809$: 30.7\% of gap ratios
    \item $\phi^{-1} \approx 0.618$: 30.7\% of gap ratios
    \item Combined $\phi^{-1} + \phi$: 52.5\% of all ratios
    \item Statistical significance: $z > 1.8\sigma$ (weak)
\end{itemize}
